About "Sock it to 'em!"

Sock it to 'em is a real time multiplayer game where the aim is to duel it out with an opponent - whoever has the most kills within the time limit wins!

Getting started

Simply head over to the home page and login. If you don't already have an account, you can register (for free) and begin playing immediately. Once you're authenticated, you'll find yourself in the lobby.

Challenging an opponent

When you're in the lobby you'll see a list of all the other players currently waiting to play, as well as a list of games currently in progress. At this point, simply click on a user's name to challenge them to a duel - before issuing the challenge you'll be given the opportunity to review their ranking to see what's at stake.

Receiving a challenge

Hang around long enough and you'll probably receive a challenge - when you do you won't be able to do anything until you've either accepted it or declined it (boo, hiss). You'll be given information about your challenger's ranking before you make your decision.

Lobby chat

If you don't fancy getting involved, you can while away the time chatting to your fellow lobby dwellers. Even if nobody else is present, you'll always have socketbot for company who'll report on any game related news.

Basics

Each game consists of a 2D, side-on view of both players and a barren wasteland between them. If you initiated the game (e.g. you're the challenger), you'll be on the left hand side. If you accepted a challenge, you'll be on the right. Using the following controls you must aim and fire your weapon in an attempt to kill your opponent as many times as possible.

Controls

In game control is handled exclusively by keyboard input. Put that mouse away!

Key Action
Up arrow Increase shot power
Down arrow Decrease shot power
Left arrow Rotate aim left
Right arrow Rotate aim right
Space bar Fire!
T Start chat mode (return key to send)
ESC Cancel chat mode

Rules

Once a game has started, the rules are pretty simple — kill your opponent as many times as possible before the time runs out! Each time you die, you will respawn on a different platform to the last, though you will never change sides. Whoever has the most kills when the timer reaches zero is declared the winner.

Sudden death

Should the scores be level when time runs out, sudden death mode will be declared. At this stage, the next person to kill their opponent wins the game.

Rankings

Game rankings are gained by defeating your opponents. What you stand to gain — or lose — depends on how your rank compares with your opponent. The following table illustrates how your ranking will change if you win or lose a game:

  You win You lose
Opponent ranked higher +3 No change
Same rank as you +2 -1
Opponent ranked lower +1 -2

As you can see, the points at stake encourage you to always take on a worthy challenger!

Technical Information

This game is developed and maintained by Payne Digital Ltd.

It is written in JavaScript - node.js powers the server side of things whilst your browser's JavaScript interpreter takes care of the client side code.

The network communication is powered by the fantastic socket.io library which leverages modern browsers' WebSockets capabilities.

The game uses no flash - just HTML5, CSS and JavaScript.
